Our Charity. Our Community

Share A Smile

Charity is a desire of the heart to help others in distress and to think good of others. It is one kind of human quality. It makes our heart bountiful. It is an expression of love for human beings and the creation of Allah. Charity comes to mitigate the sufferings of humanities. It aims at improving the condition of the poor. This quality brings happiness on earth, strengthens the ties of relationships among men, removes enmity, and creates fellow-feeling. Even a small gift can remove the sorrow of the distressed. All religions of the world encourage it.
